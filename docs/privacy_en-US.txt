Privacy Policy for Sticky Frog
Last updated: April 19, 2026

Thank you for playing Sticky Frog. This policy explains what data is processed, for what purpose, and what options you have.

1. Controller
Sticky Frog
Privacy contact: wcg.privacy@gmail.com

2. Scope
This policy applies to the Android game Sticky Frog.

3. Data we may process
A) Game data (local and gameplay)
- Progress, records, coins, unlocks, selected skins and settings.
- Technical gameplay events (for example: run start/end, run distance and duration, swing count and durations, lily-pad bounces, peak height reached, death cause and player state at death, equipped skins per run, revive usage, first-run completion, app backgrounding, shop browse summary and purchase timing).

B) Google Play Games data (if you sign in)
- Google Play Games player identifier and profile name.
- Leaderboard submissions and cloud save related metadata.

C) Advertising and monetization data (Google AdMob)
- Advertising ID / AD_ID and device identifiers used by Google advertising SDKs.
- Ad request, load, impression and interaction signals.
- Approximate location inferred by network/IP (not precise GPS from this app).

D) Analytics telemetry (Firebase Analytics native Android SDK)
- Pseudonymous app telemetry events needed to understand game performance and usage trends.
- Example fields may include: event name, timestamp, app version, session/run identifiers, gameplay metrics, shop flow and ad flow metrics.
- Pseudonymous `app_instance_id` generated by the Firebase SDK and tied to the app install (not a real-world identity, and it resets when app data is cleared or the app is reinstalled).

4. Purposes
We process data to:
- Provide core game features and local progress.
- Enable Google Play Games features (sign-in, leaderboard, cloud save).
- Show ads (interstitial and rewarded) and measure ad performance.
- Monitor stability, diagnose issues, improve balance and gameplay decisions.
- Prevent abuse, fraud and policy violations.

5. Consent and privacy choices (EEE, UK, Switzerland)
If required by law, we request consent through Google UMP (User Messaging Platform).
You can open privacy options again from the in-game Privacy button.

Important behavior in this app:
- If consent is granted or not required, ads and analytics telemetry can operate normally.
- If consent is not granted, app analytics telemetry to Firebase is not sent.
- If consent is not granted, ads may still be shown as non-personalized/limited ads, depending on Google settings and availability.

6. Third-party services
Sticky Frog uses Google services:
- Google Play Games Services
- Google AdMob
- Google Analytics for Firebase (Firebase Analytics native Android SDK embedded in the app)

Google policies apply to Google-side processing:
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Google Play Games Services Terms: https://developer.android.com/games/pgs/terms
- AdMob Policy: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

7. Data sharing
We do not sell personal data.
Data needed to provide Google Play Games and ad monetization features may be shared with Google as service provider.

8. Storage and retention
- Local game data is stored on your device and remains until you delete app data or uninstall.
- The Firebase Analytics SDK may temporarily store events on the device in order to batch and upload them when network is available, per the SDK's standard behavior.
- Google Play Games, AdMob and Firebase related data may be retained according to Google policies.
- Support requests sent by email may be retained for as long as needed to resolve your request and legal obligations.
